Physical Workout Recommendations

Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining good health. Below are the recommended times for different age groups:

Children and Adolescents (5-17 years)

Children and adolescents should engage in at least 60 minutes of moderate to vigorous intensity physical activity daily, including:

  • Aerobic: At least 3 days a week
  • Muscle-strengthening: At least 3 days a week
  • Bone-strengthening: At least 3 days a week

Adults (18-64 years)

Adults should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week, along with muscle-strengthening activities on 2 or more days a week.

Older Adults (65+ years)

Older adults with good health should follow the recommendations for adults. Those with poor mobility should do physical activity to enhance balance and prevent falls, aiming for 3 or more days per week.

Age Group Recommended Time
Children and Adolescents At least 60 minutes daily
Adults At least 150 minutes per week
Older Adults Follow adult recommendations or enhance balance
